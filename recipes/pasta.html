PASTA

Description

This classic pasta recipe is simple, quick, and delicious. Made with tomatoes, garlic, and Italian herbs, it's perfect for a satisfying meal any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti or pasta of choice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 cups chopped tomatoes (or tomato puree)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomatoes, salt, pepper, and oregano. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Toss cooked pasta in the sauce and mix well.
  5. Top with fresh basil and cheese before serving.
