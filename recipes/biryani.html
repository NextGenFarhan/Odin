Biryani

Description

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, meat, spices, and herbs. It's a popular and much-loved dish across South Asia, especially in India and Pakistan.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g Chicken (or mutton)
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 Onions (sliced)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Biryani masala
  • Fresh coriander and mint
  • Ghee or oil
  • Saffron (optional)
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Fry onions until golden, then add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until tender.
  5. Boil rice until 70% cooked, then drain.
  6. Layer chicken and rice in a pot, top with fried onions, herbs, saffron milk (optional).

  8. Serve hot with raita or salad.
