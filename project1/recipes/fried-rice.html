FRIED RICE

Description

Fried rice is a popular Indo-Chinese dish made by stir-frying rice with vegetables, soy sauce, and spices. It’s quick, flavorful, and perfect for leftover rice!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1 cup chopped mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, capsicum)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok or pan, sauté garlic until slightly golden.
  2. Add chopped vegetables and stir-fry on high heat for 2–3 minutes.
  3. Add cooked rice, soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stir well and cook for another 2–3 minutes.
  5. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
